## 🖌 Overview  
SnapMotion Advanced is a focused video frame extraction application that lets you navigate any video file with precision and capture still frames as high-resolution images, individually or in batch sets, for any downstream use.

Frame-accurate seeking lets you navigate to any point in a video with precision far beyond what a media player offers, ensuring that the frame you capture is exactly the moment you intend and not an adjacent frame.

Batch extraction automates the capture of frames at regular intervals, at every scene change, or across a manually defined list of timestamps, producing large sets of stills from any video without manual navigation.

Output quality settings control the resolution, format, and compression of exported frames, letting you produce lossless PNG files for professional use or compressed JPEG files for sharing, depending on the requirement.

For video editors who need reference stills, motion designers extracting source frames, researchers documenting video content, and photographers extracting moments from recorded video, SnapMotion Advanced is the specialized tool that handles the task with precision.
